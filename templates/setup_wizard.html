You'll need:

  • A Google account (the one you use for Gmail)
  • Access to Google Cloud Console (we'll guide you)
  • About 10-15 minutes of your time
2

Create a Google Cloud Project

First, we need to create a project in Google Cloud Console.

  • Click the button below to open Google Cloud Console
  • Sign in with your Google account if prompted
  • Click "Select a project" at the top of the page
  • Click "New Project" in the popup
  • Enter a name like "Email Pipeline" and click "Create"
  • Wait a few seconds for the project to be created
Open Google Cloud Console
3

Enable the Gmail API

Now we need to enable Gmail access for your project.

  • Click the button below to open the Gmail API page
  • Make sure your new project is selected at the top
  • Click the big blue "Enable" button
  • Wait for it to enable (takes a few seconds)
Open Gmail API Page
4

Configure OAuth Consent Screen

This tells Google what your app does.

  • Click the button below to open OAuth consent screen
  • Select "External" and click "Create"
  • Fill in the required fields:
       • App name: Email Pipeline
       • User support email: Your email
       • Developer contact: Your email (scroll down)
  • Click "Save and Continue"
  • On Scopes page, just click "Save and Continue"
  • On Test users page, click "Add Users", enter your email, then "Save and Continue"
  • Click "Back to Dashboard"
Open OAuth Consent Screen
5

Create OAuth Credentials

Now let's create the credentials and enter them here.

  • Click the button below to open the Credentials page
  • Click "Create Credentials" then "OAuth client ID"
  • For Application type, select "Web application"
  • Name it "Email Pipeline"
  • Under "Authorized redirect URIs", click "Add URI" and add the URIs shown below (click to copy each one)
  • Click "Create"
  • Copy the Client ID and Client Secret shown in the popup
Open Credentials Page

Add this Redirect URI (click to copy):

http://localhost:5000/auth/callback

Looks like: 123456789-xxxxxxxx.apps.googleusercontent.com

Looks like: GOCSPX-xxxxxxxxxxxxxx

6

Connect to Gmail

Final step! Authorize access to your Gmail account.

Click the button below to open Google's authorization page. You'll need to:

  • Sign in with your Google account
  • Click "Continue" when warned about unverified app
  • Click "Continue" again to grant permissions
  • Wait to be redirected back here
Note: You'll see a warning that the app is "unverified". This is normal for personal projects. Click "Advanced" then "Go to Email Pipeline (unsafe)" to continue.

Waiting for authorization...

Complete the authorization in the popup window
